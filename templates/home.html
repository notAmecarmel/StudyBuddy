Study Tips

Take Notes

- Clear notes today mean less stress tomorrow.

- Capture key ideas to make revision simpler and faster.

- Well-organized notes are the foundation of effective learning.

Take Breaks

- A rested mind absorbs more information.

- Short breaks can help you stay focused and refreshed.

- Pause, breathe, and refocus to maintain peak productivity.

Use Flashcards

- Quick questions, faster recall—flashcards work wonders.

- Flashcards are a great way to test your knowledge.

- Repetition with flashcards strengthens memory pathways.
